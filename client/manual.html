🚀 Getting Started

Forge Path is more than just a documentation tool; it's a laboratory for your digital ideas. Start by logging your current projects as experiments.

01

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard gives you a high-level view of your research activity, success rates, and your current tier ranking. The activity heatmap shows your daily contributions over the last year.

🧪 Adding Experiments

Every time you try a new library, database, or architectural pattern, log it as an experiment. This builds your personal knowledge base over time.

02

Logging Details

Include the technology name, a clear hypothesis (what you expect to happen), and the actual outcome. Success or failure doesn't matter as much as the why.

💡

Pro Tip

Be specific about versions. "React 18" is better than just "React" for future reference.

⚖️ Comparing Technology

Use the "Compare" feature to put two experiments side-by-side. This is ideal for deciding between similar tools (e.g., Axios vs Fetch, or Postgres vs MongoDB). Each comparison earns you XP!

🏆 XP & Badges

As you log more experiments and reach milestones, you'll earn XP and unlock badges. Your rank progresses through tiers: Novice → Apprentice → Adept → Expert → Master.

🎖️

Badge Milestones

First Blood (1 experiment) • High Five (5 experiments) • Perfect Ten (10 experiments) • Analyst (use Compare tool)

✨ Pro Tips

Get the most out of Forge Path with these power-user strategies.

🔥

Build Streaks

Log at least one activity per day to build your streak. Streaks help you maintain consistent learning habits.

📊

Use Tags Wisely

Tag experiments with technology categories to easily filter and find related research later.
